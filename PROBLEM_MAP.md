# Problem-to-Feature Map: Antardrishti Mind

Every feature in **Antardrishti Mind** is designed to address a specific psychological or academic gap experienced by competitive exam aspirants (JEE, NEET, UPSC, etc.).

| Identified Gap in Student Life | Feature in Antardrishti Mind | How it Addresses the Gap |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Chronically High Stress & Overwhelm** | AI Reflective Journal (`MindSpace.tsx`) | Allows expressing thoughts (via text or voice dictation) and translates them into an objective emotional analysis. |
| **Exam Anxiety / Low Confidence** | Wellness Coach Recommendations (`MindSpace.tsx`, `Home.tsx`) | Offers low-pressure, micro-steps tailored to the student's current stress and anxiety metrics. |
| **Undetected Stress Cycles** | Hidden Trigger Detection (`MindSpace.tsx`) | Automatically analyzes journal entries over time to identify recurring stressors (e.g. mock test drop -> anxiety spike). |
| **Physical Burnout & Neglect** | Brain Fuel Score & Log (`BodyBrain.tsx`) | Computes a single daily score based on Sleep, Hydration, Activity, and Nutrition, identifying direct drivers. |
| **Academic Focus Food Confusion** | Nutrition Intelligence (`BodyBrain.tsx`) | Recommends specific focus foods or calming tea based on current emotional states (e.g., Magnesium for anxiety). |
| **Physical Panic / Exam Day Stress** | Pranayama Guide (`BodyBrain.tsx`) | Interactive, Socratic alternate nostril (Anulom Vilom) and Humming (Bhramari) breathing pacers to calm the autonomic nervous system. |
| **Distraction & Screen Fatigue** | Pomodoro & Digital Detox (`BodyBrain.tsx`) | A 25/5 focus timer that serves digital detox prompts to rest eyes and step away from social media. |
| **Overwhelming Syllabus Load** | Syllabus Prioritizer (`ExamPath.tsx`) | Allows students to track and prioritize topics based on difficulty and weightage. |
| **Inefficient Exam Prep** | PYQ Intelligence & Resource recommender (`ExamPath.tsx`) | Surfaces historical high-yield topic weightages and recommends standard textbooks to avoid resource confusion. |
| **Sub-optimal Study Habits** | Peak Performance Analytics (`ExamPath.tsx`) | Charts study session focus efficiency to derive the student's best hours of focus and top subject efficiencies. |
| **Identity Tied Only to Exam Marks** | Purpose Engine (`BeyondMarks.tsx`) | Persistent "Why" statement and Future Self Visualization to ground self-worth in character, not just scores. |
| **Fear of Total Failure** | Parallel Pathways Explorer (`BeyondMarks.tsx`) | Maps clear, rewarding career pathways for both clearing and not clearing the target exam (neither is framed as a consolation prize). |
| **No Tangible Sense of Progress** | Growth Tree & Resilience Score (`BeyondMarks.tsx`) | An SVG-based growth tree that grows leaves for every logged activity (journaling, breathing, studying) to visual effort over outcomes. |
| **Parental Expectation Pressures** | Parent Connect AI Summary (`FamilyBridge.tsx`) | Translates emotional distress into parent-facing updates and suggestions, protecting student journal privacy. |
| **Inability to Talk to Parents** | Family Discussion Assistant (`FamilyBridge.tsx`) | Scripted conversation templates with steering tips for discussing burnout, mock test drops, or career backups. |
| **Immediate Crisis Panic** | Persistent SOS Support (`SOSModal.tsx`) | 1-tap reachable modal displaying official Indian helplines (Tele MANAS, KIRAN) with direct dial links. |
| **Unproductive Doubts & Exam Stuckness** | Doubt Buddy Socratic Tutor (`DoubtBuddy.tsx`) | Socratic academic chat assistant that guides through step-by-step concepts rather than spoon-feeding final answers. |
| **Student Privacy Leakage / Account Safety** | Local-Only Identity Model (`SettingsPage.tsx`) | 100% login-free. Stored in IndexedDB. No credentials, no data transmission of PII to central servers. |
| **High Stress Information Overload** | Calm Mode (`SettingsPage.tsx`, `App.tsx`) | A 1-tap accessibility option to reduce colors, visual motion, contrast levels, and details for calm focus. |
| **Aspirants from Diverse Regions** | Multilingual Support (`translations.ts`) | Toggleable UI translations for English, Hindi, and Tamil. |
| **Reading Accessibility Barriers** | Dyslexia-Friendly Typography (`SettingsPage.tsx`) | A font override option to switch body typefaces to high-legibility OpenDyslexic characters. |
